About Nativity

We believe that truly glowing skin comes from a serenity borne out of respect for oneself and the world we inhabit. We hope that choosing Nativity inspires you to go beyond non-toxic beauty and seek the benefits of simplicity, wellness and balance in all aspects of life.

The body is a temple, a sacred place to be treated mindfully and what touches our skin, seeps within, restoring serenity, purity and balance to the centre of our world. This is why we sourced products with a simple regimen of multi-use beauty essentials, handcrafted with simple natural ingredients from the Lushlands of Africa, to keep your body pure and your skin glowing.

Rooted in wellness traditions from Africa, Nativity takes a less is more approach to beauty. Nativity's carefully sourced products are free from sulfates, parabens, phthalates, silicones, mineral oil and other harmful ingredients. We also want you to have a choice, so each beauty essential comes in a fragrance free variant.

Are your products suitable for all skin types?

Yes. Dry, oily, combo – we got you. For very sensitive skin, you can use all our products.

Are your products suitable for all skin tones?

YES. Beauty has no skin tone.

Do you deliver worldwide?

We offer worldwide shipping to all countries around the world with an estimated delivery time frame of 5 – 10 working days.

Are your products reusable?

Yes! You can reuse our glass jars in your home in various ways, such as storage. We also offer a return and refill service.

How long will it take to see results?

Every skin type is different, but most of our customers have seen results after about 2 weeks, so we are hopeful that you will have the same experience. We however make no guarantees.
